To use Magix Music Maker 2023, your PC must meet the following minimum specifications:

* **Windows:** 10/11 64-bit
* **Processor:** 2 GHz
* **RAM:** 2 GB
* **Graphics Card:** Onboard, at least 1280 x 768 resolution
